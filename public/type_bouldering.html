Modern Rock Climbing

Bouldering

  • Bouldering "problems," as they're named, are usually short, often only a few meters high, utilizing powerful technical movements.
  • Climbers use crash pads as protection from falls and chalk to keep their hands dry for a better grip.
  • Bouldering is a popular form of climbing due to its accessibility and minimal gear involved.
  • Most indoor climbing facilities have a large square footage dedicated to bouldering.
