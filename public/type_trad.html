Traditional Climbing

  • Trad climbing is a form of rock climbing where climbers place removable protection devices as they ascend.
  • Cultivates a strong sense of connection between climbers and the natural environment, emphasizing the importance of respecting the rock and the surrounding ecosystem.
  • Promotes exploration by encouraging climbers to seek out uncharted routes and connect with the history and heritage of the climbing locations, fostering a deep appreciation for the cultural significance of the terrain.
